About

Birgit Bauer Wöhlers is a specialist in cellular optimization, advanced aesthetics, and integrative health, with over a decade of experience across international strategy, wellness, and cross-sector collaboration.

Her work focuses on designing and curating systems that redefine how individuals and organizations approach health, performance, and long-term vitality, integrating science, aesthetics, and environment into cohesive and elevated ways of living.

She is the founder of Kalos, a company dedicated to exploring innovations in longevity, biotechnology, and regenerative wellbeing, while developing concepts that translate emerging science into refined, real-world applications.

With a background in international relations, public policy, and strategic advisory, Birgit brings a structured yet intuitive approach to wellbeing, bridging disciplines such as integrative health, environmental design, and human behavior.

Her work extends across the United States, the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas, collaborating with leaders and initiatives shaping the future of longevity, conscious living, and human development.
