What I Do

I curate environments, strategies, and experiences that support wellbeing, longevity, and the quality of how we live.

My work examines how lifestyle, space, beauty, and emerging science come together to shape vitality, mental clarity, resilience, and long-term health.

The environment is active. It shapes how the body functions, how cells express themselves, and how we age. At the scale of a room, a program, or an institution, designing with intention is where longevity strategy begins.

Kalokagathia

Kalos kai agathos

Beauty and excellence, held together.

In classical thought, kalokagathia referred to the cultivated human being: physical refinement, inner virtue, disciplined conduct, and the qualities associated with noble leadership.

This is the philosophy behind KALOS and AGATHOS: two expressions of one idea, where living well is understood through the body we cultivate, the environments we shape, and the quality of what we become.

About

Birgit Bauer Wöhlers, NMD, is the founder of KALOS, a longevity innovation venture established in 2015, and AGATHOS, a private advisory practice.

Her work brings together cellular optimization, advanced aesthetics, integrative health, environmental policy, wellbeing design, and strategic advisory, with current work extending into hospitality, wellness ecosystems and longevity frameworks across the Gulf, Europe, and the Americas.
